Our Services


Industrial product design or industrial design is to design new and up-to-date products for mass production by considering various criteria such as aesthetics, creativity, technical advantage, functionality, ergonomics, material knowledge, marketability, production methods and possibilities.

In line with the demands of its business partners, 312 Makina produces solutions such as Industrial Design Process Optimization that will make the Production and Manufacturing processes efficient.


Metalworking is the work done to give the desired shape to the metal with cnc machines and various benches. The most important and easy tool for metalworking is the cnc machine. The metal processing industry is a sector that is developing day by day and is the backbone of the industry.

It offers solutions to its business partners regarding their needs with Laser Cutting, Abkant Bending, Cylindrical Bending Machine Park.


Welded Manufacturing is the preferred manufacturing method due to the reasons such as material wastage cost and cost reduction in projects that require high strength according to the nature of the projects, that it is not possible or suitable to be combined with bolts, and that are drawn in non-standard dimensions.

With our defense industry approved welded manufacturing capability, we become a solution partner in all welded manufacturing times that business partners need.
